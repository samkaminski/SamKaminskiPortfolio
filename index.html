About Me

Hi! I'm a Junior studying Computer Science at the University of Michigan, and I'm set to graduate in 2026 with a Bachelor's degree. I'm interested in exploring fields like Software Development, Technical Consulting, and Technical Sales. I'm always eager to broaden my horizons by trying new things, gaining experience in different areas, and meeting new people. This semester, I'm taking two Computer Science courses: EECS 376, which covers the theory of computation, including topics like Turing machines, decidability, NP-hardness, and cryptography, and EECS 370, which focuses on computer organization, assembly language, processor performance, and more. Most of my technical experience is in C++, C, HTML, CSS, and R, but by the end of this year, I'm hoping to expand my skills in Python, SQL, and Machine Learning.

This semester, I joined a campus student organization called Michigan Hackers, and I'm super excited to be part of the Open-Source Development team. On this team, I've been able to deepen my understanding of large codebases and sharpen my teamwork skills by contributing to complex open-source projects. Working with diverse teams has also improved my ability to manage projects efficiently. I'm really looking forward to the rest of the year with this group!
